About Me

Hello.

My name is Trixia, and I bring 6 years of experience as a graphic designer, content creator, video editor, Project Manager, and copywriting. I've honed my skills in creating visually captivating designs, managing online platforms like Etsy, and effectively handling social media campaigns. Designing is not just a profession for me. I express my creativity and bring ideas to life.

Beyond design, I am passionate about photography, capturing special moments and conveying stories through my lens. I'm also the host of the podcast "Let's Talk About Random Things," where I engage listeners in intriguing conversations on various topics. Additionally, I have a deep love for travel, constantly seeking new experiences and opportunities to broaden my skill set.

From building Etsy stores from scratch to managing full digital marketing campaigns, I bring strategy and soul to every project I touch.
